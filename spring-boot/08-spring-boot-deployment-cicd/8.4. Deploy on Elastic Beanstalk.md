> **Modern alternatives to consider:** Elastic Beanstalk is on a long deprecation/decline trend in 2026. For new deployments most teams reach for:
>
> - **AWS App Runner**: simplest managed service for containerized Spring Boot apps; closest to Beanstalk's "just give me a deploy" experience
> - **AWS ECS Fargate**: production default for serious workloads; container-native, auto-scaling, integrates with ECR + ALB + CloudWatch
> - **AWS EKS**: Kubernetes when you need cross-cloud portability or already use K8s elsewhere
>
> Beanstalk is taught here as a low-friction managed entry point, but evaluate ECS Fargate for new prod deployments.
